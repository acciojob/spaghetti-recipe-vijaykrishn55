Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
  3. Add salt to taste.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
  6. Mix cooked spaghetti with marinara sauce.
  7. Enjoy!
